Laptop Lending Process

Request Submission:

Students fill out a laptop request form, either online or in-person, providing necessary details like student ID, course information, and the duration for which the laptop is needed.

Verification and Approval:

After receiving the request, university staff verify the student's eligibility based on criteria such as enrollment status, academic standing, and availability of laptops. Once verified, the request is approved.

Laptop Pickup:

Upon approval, students are notified via email or SMS about the pickup location and time. They can then go to the designated area, show their student ID, and receive the laptop for the approved loan period.
